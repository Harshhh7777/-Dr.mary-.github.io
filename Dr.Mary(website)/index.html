About Me

As a Doctor of Pharmacy graduate with an M.Sc in Nutrition & Dietetics, Dr Mary Raju Mathew brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the realm of whole food plant-based nutrition and lifestyle medicine.
Certified in Plant-based Nutrition by the T. Colin Campbell Center, she also holds certifications in Natural Health Science from the Institute of Maha Yoga and Natural Hygiene (IMANAH) and is a registered RYT-200 yoga teacher. With her expertise, she empowers individuals to take charge of their health, becoming the captains of their own well-being.
Inspired by the teachings of Acharya K Lakshmana Sarma, the esteemed Father of Nature Cure in India, Nature Cure also known as Natural Hygiene, embraces the timeless wisdom of Nature's Laws, recognizing their transformative power in our lives. Central to the Nature Cure philosophy is the fascinating interplay between our genes and the five fold food, which are the five elements of nature—the space, air, fire, water and earth. In this intricate dance of existence, these elements communicate with our genes, shaping not only our physical health but also our mental and spiritual well-being.
